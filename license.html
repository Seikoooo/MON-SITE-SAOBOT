Single user restriction

You receive a non-transferable, non-exclusive license to run SAOBOT on one Windows device at a time. Additional PCs require additional licenses. Virtual machines, cloud PCs, and streaming setups are prohibited unless expressly approved by SAOBOT staff.

2. Reverse engineering

No tampering

Decompiling, sniffing traffic, modifying binaries, or sharing loaders/cracks is forbidden. Violations trigger immediate termination without refund and permanent bans from support channels.

3. Updates & support

Continuous delivery

Active licenses include all minor updates and anti-cheat bypass maintenance. Access to beta features may be revoked at any time if stability, compliance, or risk scores degrade.

4. Termination

Causes of suspension

Licenses may be terminated for fraudulent payments, chargebacks, harassment of staff, leaks of private builds, or breach of the Arduino hardware requirement. Terminated accounts lose access immediately and cannot re-register without written approval.
